**Claim.** Families of probability distributions form curved manifolds with
natural metrics (Fisher information) and dual connections; statistical
structure is intrinsically geometric.

**Relation.** Convergent. Arithmon also treats informational structure as
geometric.

**Δ.** Information geometry stays at the level of statistical manifolds and is
agnostic about fundamental physics; Arithmon asks whether the dimensionless
constants themselves are exact topological counts of one compact geometry.
Amari's program has decades of theorems and applications behind it; Arithmon's
reading of the constants is a young, falsifiable bet.
