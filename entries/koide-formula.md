**Claim.** The charged lepton masses satisfy an exact-looking algebraic
relation, (m_e + m_mu + m_tau) divided by the square of the sum of the square
roots equals 2/3, to striking precision and with no accepted derivation.

**Relation.** Convergent in spirit: an exact algebraic relation among
measured constants, taken seriously for decades.

**Δ.** Koide is one isolated relation without a structural origin, orphaned
for forty years; Arithmon embeds many relations in a single declared
vocabulary with set-level statistics and a geometric origin candidate. Koide
is also the cautionary tale the program must answer: exactness alone does not
make a theory, and longevity without an origin is a possible fate.
