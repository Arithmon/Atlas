**Claim.** Geometry leaves quantitative signatures in spectra; part of a
manifold's shape, though not all of it, can be recovered from its eigenvalues.

**Relation.** Convergent. The prediction chain of Arithmon's founding
framework runs through Laplacian spectra of the compact geometry.

**Δ.** Inverse spectral geometry asks what spectra reveal about geometry in
general; Arithmon makes the converse, physical bet: that the sound of one
compact geometry is not merely qualitative but numerically identical to the
measured constants of nature.
