**Claim.** Physics may emerge from discrete computational rewriting rules on
hypergraphs; space, time and matter are downstream of rule dynamics.

**Relation.** Orthogonal, with a partial divergence on mechanism.

**Δ.** Wolfram seeks emergence of physical law from computation and treats
specific constants as largely out of reach for now; Arithmon is not
computational emergence: it is arithmetic-geometric determination, reading
specific dimensionless constants directly as topological invariants of a
fixed geometry.
